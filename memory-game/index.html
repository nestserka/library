Lucky's whack a cancer game

What is this game about?

Have you heard about Whac-A-Mole game? The rules are simple: your goal is to whack a mole fast when his hat lights up, and/or he makes his special sound.

This summer my dog (his name is Lucky, but seems he is not that lucky) got cancer. So instead of hitting the mole, I decided to change the game a bit.

In the given time your goal is to hit as many cancer-cells as only you can and save Lucky. Each hit will bring you 2 points.

You must score at least 30 points and get the highest to win this game!
